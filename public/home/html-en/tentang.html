What is NikiLa POL

NikiLa Online Market Lamongan or abbreviated Nikila POL is a Super Apps that combines Marketplace and Delivery Order, which has the aim of being a place for selling local wisdom products of Lamongan Regency and to increase the Regency's PAD (Regional Original Income).

Why should NikiLa POL ?

Regional Product Portfolio

NikiLa POL is an E-Catalog of local wisdom products of Lamongan Regency which is a portfolio of regional products for the MSME, commodity, and tourism sectors, making it easier for tourists to recognize the products of Lamongan Regency local wisdom.
