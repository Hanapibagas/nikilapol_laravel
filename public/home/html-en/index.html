About

What is NikiLa POL

NikiLa Online Market Lamongan or abbreviated Nikila POL is a Super Apps that combines Marketplace dan Delivery Order, which has the aim of being a place for selling local wisdom products of Lamongan Regency and to increase the Regency's PAD (Regional Original Income).

Facilities We Serve

Why should NikiLa POL ?

Regional Product Portfolio

NikiLa POL is an E-Catalog of local wisdom products of Lamongan Regency which is a portfolio of regional products for the MSME, commodity, and tourism sectors, making it easier for tourists to recognize the products of Lamongan Regency local wisdom.

What do we offer?

Services that can accompany your day

Transportation & Logistics

We provide local online transportation and logistics services to facilitate the process of sending products to the Lamongan community.

Food & Shopping

A feature service for buyers who want to shop for Lamongan products.

Tour

Service features to guide tourists to get to know tourist destinations in Lamongan Regency, so they can make reservations and order travel tickets digitally.

Payment

A safe and reliable payment service to ensure payment security in the NikiLa POL application.

Working capital

Financing services for NikiLa POL partners, especially productive UMKM and UMKM partners.
